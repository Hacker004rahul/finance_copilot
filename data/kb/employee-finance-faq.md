# NovaLedger Inc. Employee Finance FAQ

## Q1. How do I submit an expense?
Submit it in LedgerLoop with a receipt, category, cost center, and business purpose within 10 calendar days.

## Q2. When are reimbursements paid?
Approved reimbursements are paid on the 7th and 22nd of each month, or the next business day.

## Q3. What is the approval limit for my manager?
A direct manager may approve expenses up to 250 USD.

## Q4. Who approves an expense above 5,000 USD?
CFO Priya Raman must approve any expense above 5,000 USD before purchase.

## Q5. Can I expense a personal gym membership?
No. Personal gym memberships are prohibited expenses.

## Q6. Can I buy a SaaS subscription myself?
Use the SaaS intake form first. Team trials up to 90 USD per user per month may be manager-approved for three months.

## Q7. What if I lost a receipt?
For expenses under 25 USD you may sign a missing receipt declaration; otherwise reimbursement is delayed.

## Q8. Can I fly premium economy?
Premium economy is allowed for flights of six hours or more with director approval.

## Q9. What is the hotel cap?
The cap is 260 USD per night in standard cities and 340 USD in high-cost cities.

## Q10. What is the daily meal cap?
The daily meal cap is 75 USD: 18 USD breakfast, 22 USD lunch, and 35 USD dinner.

## Q11. Can I expense alcohol?
Only at approved customer events, and it must be itemized separately.

## Q12. When should overspend be escalated?
Escalate when expected overspend is more than 5 percent or 25,000 USD above the quarterly budget.

## Q13. What needs a purchase order?
New vendor requests above 15,000 USD per year require Procurement review and a purchase order.

## Q14. How long does Finance keep exception records?
Finance Operations keeps exception records for seven years.

## Q15. Can I use a corporate card for cash advances?
No. Corporate cards may not be used for cash advances.

## Q16. What happens if a receipt is late?
A card transaction over 1,500 USD without a receipt can trigger an automatic card hold after seven days.
